R1Speed Limits 速度限制

Sources: The Highway Code Rules 124–126 · Road Traffic Regulation Act 1984 s.81–89 · Motor Vehicles (Variation of Speed Limits) (England & Wales) Regulations 2014

Speed limits depend on vehicle type AND road type. Learn this table — it is heavily tested.

速度限制視乎車種同道路類型。考試常問，必須背熟。
⚠ Streetlights = 30 mph
If there are streetlights and no speed signs, the limit is 30 mph (built-up area). 市區有街燈而無標示 = 默認30英里/小時。

National Speed Limit Sign 國家速度限制標誌

A white circle with a single black diagonal stripe means the national speed limit applies — what that limit is depends on your vehicle and the road type (see table above).

白底圓形 + 一條黑色斜線 = 「國家標準限速」。實際速度看車種同路型決定。

⚠ Speed Limit Is a MAXIMUM
It is the upper limit, not a target. Drive slower if conditions (weather, traffic, visibility) require. Exceeding the limit = endorsement + fine. 限速係上限，唔係目標。情況惡劣要更慢。
