Stopping distances appear in many test questions. Memorise the totals and the rule of thumb. 停車距離係考試熱門題目，必須背熟。

💡 Memory Trick · 記憶法

Multiply the speed (in mph) by these factors to get total stopping distance in feet, then convert:

  • 20 mph × 2 = 40 ft · 30 mph × 2.5 = 75 ft · 40 mph × 3 = 120 ft
  • 50 mph × 3.5 = 175 ft · 60 mph × 4 = 240 ft · 70 mph × 4.5 = 315 ft

Or memorise car lengths: 3, 6, 9, 13, 18, 24. 記住車身數：3, 6, 9, 13, 18, 24

The Two-Second Rule 兩秒法則

In good, dry conditions, leave at least 2 seconds between you and the vehicle in front. Pick a fixed point (lamp post, sign), and when the car ahead passes it, say:

"Only a fool breaks the two-second rule" — takes about 2 seconds to say.
「Only a fool breaks the two-second rule」唸完一次 ≈ 2秒，若你過點之前未講完，距離太近。
  • Wet conditions: at least 4 seconds (double)
  • Icy conditions: at least 20 seconds (ten times)
