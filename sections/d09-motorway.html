D9Motorway Rules 高速公路規則

Sources: The Highway Code Rules 253–273 · Motorways Traffic (England & Wales) Regulations 1982 · DfT Smart Motorways: Safety Evidence Stocktake 2020

Motorways are the fastest, most strictly regulated UK roads. Special rules apply.

高速公路速度最快，規則最嚴。學車前必須清楚。

Who Cannot Use Motorways 不准使用高速公路

  • Learner drivers — except in a car with dual controls accompanied by an approved instructor
  • Pedestrians
  • Cyclists (pedal cycles)
  • Motorcycles under 50cc (and mopeds)
  • Certain agricultural vehicles (tractors etc.)
  • Horse riders, animals
  • Invalid carriages under 254kg unladen

Lane Discipline 行車線紀律

  • Keep to the LEFT lane unless overtaking. The left lane is the driving lane on a 3-lane motorway.
  • Middle and right lanes are for overtaking only — return to the left lane when done.
  • Lane hogging (staying in middle/right unnecessarily) is an offence — fixed penalty £100 + 3 points.
  • Do not overtake on the left (undertaking) — exception: in slow-moving congested traffic when your lane is moving faster.
沒事就靠左行，超車後要返回左線。長期霸住中線或快線會被罰款並扣分。

Joining and Leaving 入閘同出閘

Joining

  • Use the slip road to build up speed to match motorway traffic
  • Give way to traffic already on the motorway
  • Signal, check mirrors, check blind spot, merge into the left lane
  • Stay in left lane until adjusted to speed (usually a few hundred metres)

Leaving

  • Watch for countdown markers (300/200/100 yards before exit)
  • Signal left in good time
  • Move to the left lane well before exit
  • Reduce speed on the slip road — speedometer "blindness" makes you feel slow when really still fast

Hard Shoulder & Breakdowns 硬路肩同故障處理

⚠ Hard Shoulder — Emergencies ONLY
Hard shoulder is for genuine emergencies only. Stopping for a phone call, toilet break, or to check directions is illegal.

If you break down on a motorway:

  1. Try to leave at the next exit or services if possible
  2. If you must stop: pull fully onto hard shoulder, as far left as possible, wheels turned left
  3. Switch on hazard lights
  4. Exit the vehicle from the LEFT (passenger) side
  5. Stand behind the safety barrier, away from traffic
  6. Use the emergency telephone (free, points to nearest control centre) or call 999. Arrows on marker posts (every 100m) point to the nearest phone.
  7. Do NOT put out a warning triangle on a motorway hard shoulder (too dangerous)
  8. Wear high-visibility clothing if available
  9. Keep animals in the vehicle (unless emergency)
壞車時：開警示燈 → 由左門落車 → 企喺護欄外 → 用緊急電話或999求救。千祈唔好喺硬路肩擺警示三角架，太危險。

Smart Motorways 智能高速公路

Modern motorways use technology to manage traffic. Three types:

  • Controlled motorway — variable speed limits but normal hard shoulder remains
  • Dynamic Hard Shoulder (DHS) — hard shoulder open as a running lane at busy times
  • All Lane Running (ALR) — no permanent hard shoulder; emergency refuge areas (ERAs) every ~1.5 miles

Gantry Signs 電子龍門牌

  • Red X over a lane = lane closed. Do NOT drive in that lane — it could be hiding an accident or stopped vehicle. 紅X = 該線封閉，絕對不可進入。
  • Speed in a red circle = mandatory variable speed limit
  • Blank gantry / no speed = national speed limit applies (70 mph)
⚠ Red X on Smart Motorway
Driving in a lane marked with a Red X = fixed penalty (£100 + 3 points) and possibly court action. Could put lives at risk.

Fog & Visibility 霧與能見度

Switch on fog lights when visibility drops below 100 metres. Switch them OFF when visibility improves (dazzles other drivers, masks brake lights).

能見度低於100米先可以開霧燈，能見度回復就要關。

Contraflow Systems

Roadworks may create contraflows: opposing traffic uses the same carriageway. Reduce speed, keep extra distance, no overtaking unless safe.
