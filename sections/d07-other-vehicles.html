D7Sharing the Road with Other Vehicles 與其他車輛共用道路

Sources: The Highway Code Rules 163, 219–225 (motorcycles/cyclists) · Rules 291–292 (trams) · Rules 219–225 (large vehicles) · Rules 194 (emergency vehicles)

Large Vehicles (HGVs, Buses, Coaches) 大型車輛

  • Large blind spots — if you can't see the driver's mirrors, they probably can't see you
  • Turn wide — they may move to the right to make a left turn, or vice versa. Don't try to overtake when a large vehicle is positioning to turn
  • When overtaking: takes longer; check what's ahead carefully
  • After overtaking: don't cut in immediately — they need long stopping distances
  • Spray from HGVs in wet conditions reduces visibility
大車有巨大死角，看唔到司機鏡 = 司機都睇唔到你。大車轉彎要兜大彎，唔好以為佢撳左轉燈又走右邊就係玩你。

Emergency Vehicles 緊急車輛

Sirens + flashing blue lights (ambulance, fire engine, police), or green (doctor's car):

  • Pull over safely — don't panic-brake or swerve
  • Try to find a safe spot on the left to let them pass
  • Do NOT stop in narrow streets where they can't pass
  • Do NOT mount kerbs, drive on pavements, run red lights — even to make way
  • If you can't move safely, signal them to overtake

Trams 電車

  • Trams run on fixed rails — they cannot steer around obstacles
  • Almost silent — easy to miss
  • Give them priority where possible
  • Watch for tram stops in the middle of the road — passengers cross to/from
  • Steel rails are slippery, especially in wet — avoid sudden braking/steering on rails
  • Some signs apply only to tram drivers (white-on-black)

Buses 巴士

  • Give way to buses pulling out from bus stops when safe — required when bus signals to pull away
  • Watch for passengers crossing in front of / behind buses
  • Bus lanes: check the times shown on the sign — entering during operating hours = fine
  • School buses: special attention; children getting on/off may dash across road

Motorcycles 電單車

  • Check mirrors AND blind spots — especially before changing lanes/turning
  • Motorcyclists "filter" between lanes in heavy traffic — be aware
  • Most accidents at junctions — drivers often fail to spot them
  • Watch motorcyclists carefully in wet weather / strong wind — they may be blown about
  • Allow extra space when overtaking

Slow-Moving / Agricultural Vehicles 慢速車輛

  • Tractors, road sweepers, gritters — be patient
  • Overtake only when safe and you can see well ahead
  • Tractors may have flashing amber light
  • Animals being herded — slow right down, follow farmer's directions

Convoys & Funeral Processions 車隊及送葬隊伍

  • Don't break up a funeral procession by overtaking
  • Be patient — they move slowly out of respect
