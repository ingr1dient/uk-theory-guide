D15Environmental Issues 環境問題

Sources: The Highway Code Rules 123–124 · DfT Eco-driving guidance · Clean Air Zones Framework (JAQU 2019) · Environment Act 2021

Eco-Driving (Driving Efficiently) 慳油駕駛

  • Accelerate gently — harsh acceleration uses much more fuel
  • Anticipate traffic and decelerate early — avoid harsh braking
  • Maintain steady speeds — use cruise control on motorways
  • Change to higher gears early — modern engines pull from low revs
  • Keep tyres properly inflated — under-inflation increases rolling resistance and fuel use
  • Remove roof racks/boxes when not used (drag = more fuel)
  • Don't carry unnecessary weight
  • Avoid short, cold-start journeys — engine is most inefficient when cold
  • Plan journeys to combine errands; avoid peak traffic
節能駕駛重點：溫柔加速、預早減速、保持均速、早升波。輪胎要打夠氣，行李廂唔需要嘅嘢清走。

Emissions & Pollutants 排放與污染物

  • Carbon monoxide (CO) — toxic, colourless, odourless
  • Nitrogen oxides (NOx) — contribute to smog, respiratory illness
  • Hydrocarbons (HC) — unburnt fuel; smog precursor
  • Particulate matter (PM) — diesel soot; lung damage
  • CO₂ — greenhouse gas, contributes to climate change

Catalytic Converter 觸媒轉化器

Reduces harmful gases by converting them through chemical reactions (e.g., CO → CO₂, NOx → N₂). Needs to be hot to work, so most pollution occurs in the first few minutes after a cold start.

Euro Emissions Standards 歐盟排放標準

Number from Euro 1 (oldest) to Euro 6 (current). Higher number = lower emissions = cleaner. Used to assess if a vehicle can enter Clean Air Zones, ULEZ, etc.

Low Emission Zones (LEZ) & Clean Air Zones (CAZ) 低排放區

  • Areas where higher-emission (older) vehicles are charged or banned
  • London ULEZ (Ultra Low Emission Zone): operates 24/7 in much of Greater London
  • Other cities: Birmingham, Bristol, Bath, Portsmouth, Sheffield, etc.
  • Cars with Euro 4 petrol (~2005+) or Euro 6 diesel (~2015+) usually exempt

Engine Idling 引擎怠速

  • Avoid idling unnecessarily — wastes fuel and pollutes
  • If stationary for over a minute, switch off engine (or let stop-start system do it)
  • Idling near schools / homes is an offence in many areas (£20–£80 fine)

Air Conditioning 冷氣

  • Increases fuel consumption by ~8% — turn off when not needed
  • At low speeds, open windows; at high speeds, AC more efficient than open windows (drag)
  • Demisting windscreen: AC helps dry the air faster than vents alone

Other Eco Tips 其他環保建議

  • Service vehicle regularly — keeps engine efficient
  • Use unleaded petrol grade recommended by manufacturer
  • Dispose of used oil and batteries at authorised sites (NEVER drains/bins)
  • Consider car sharing, public transport, cycling, walking for short trips
