Tyres 輪胎

⚠ Legal Minimum Tread Depth
1.6 mm across the central three-quarters (¾) of the tread, around the entire circumference. Penalty: up to £2,500 + 3 points PER TYRE.
輪胎合法最低胎紋深度：1.6毫米。每條不合格輪胎可罰款£2,500 + 3分。
  • Check pressure when tyres are cold (not after long drive)
  • Under-inflated tyres → more fuel use, uneven wear, blowouts
  • Over-inflated tyres → bumpy ride, less grip, centre wear
  • Look for cuts, bulges, embedded objects regularly
  • Spare tyre — should also be checked; many cars now use repair kits or run-flats

Lights 車燈

LightWhen to use
SidelightsFrom sunset to sunrise on roads with streetlights; helps others see you
Dipped headlightsWhen visibility is reduced; built-up areas at night; dawn/dusk
Full beam (high beam)Dark roads with no street lighting; dip when oncoming traffic within 200m or following close
Fog lights (front)Visibility < 100m; switch OFF when clears
Fog lights (rear)Visibility < 100m; mandatory if so equipped; OFF when clears
Hazard lightsStationary & warning of hazard; brief use on motorway/dual carriageway to warn of sudden congestion
Reversing lightsCome on automatically in reverse — warn others
Brake lightsActivate automatically; check working before any journey

Engine & Fluid Checks 引擎及液體檢查

  • Engine oil: check with dipstick weekly or before long journeys (between MIN and MAX)
  • Coolant: between MIN and MAX in expansion tank; only check when engine cold
  • Brake fluid: between MIN and MAX; if low, do NOT drive — possible leak
  • Power steering fluid: if heavy steering or whining noise
  • Windscreen washer fluid: top up with screenwash (NOT washing-up liquid — damages paintwork)
  • Battery: check terminals clean and tight

Brakes 煞車系統

  • Disc brakes: usually on front wheels (sometimes all four)
  • Drum brakes: usually rear; older / cheaper vehicles
  • ABS (Anti-lock Braking System): prevents wheel lock-up under hard braking
  • With ABS: press the brake pedal firmly and keep pressure on — do NOT pump
  • ABS does not shorten braking distance on all surfaces — it helps you steer while braking
  • ABS warning light on while driving = system fault — get it checked
  • Spongy/soft pedal = air in system or fluid leak — danger
ABS（防鎖死煞車系統）：用力踩煞車不要泵。ABS主要功能係讓你邊煞邊轉軚，唔一定縮短停車距離。

Windscreen & Visibility 擋風玻璃

  • Must be clear of ice, snow, condensation BEFORE setting off (offence to drive with impaired view)
  • Chips larger than 10mm in driver's view = MOT failure
  • Wiper blades: replace when smearing
  • Demister: use to clear condensation; aircon helps dry the air faster

MOT Test MOT 年檢

  • Required from 3 years after first registration, then annually
  • Certificate valid 12 months
  • Driving without valid MOT: fine up to £1,000, no insurance, no road tax renewal
  • Exception: driving TO a pre-booked MOT test is allowed
  • MOT checks: brakes, steering, lights, exhaust, tyres, seatbelts, mirrors, wipers, body, suspension, emissions
  • MOT does NOT check engine, clutch, gearbox condition

Seatbelts 安全帶

  • Driver and ALL passengers must wear seatbelts where fitted
  • Driver responsible for under-14s wearing correct restraint
  • Children under 12 years OR under 135cm: must use child seat / booster appropriate for weight
  • Exemptions: holders of medical exemption certificate; reversing; some delivery drivers within prescribed distances
  • Penalty for no seatbelt: £100 fixed penalty, up to £500 in court
⚠ Airbags & Child Seats
NEVER put a rear-facing child seat in a front passenger seat with an active airbag. The airbag deploys with enormous force and can kill an infant. Deactivate airbag or use rear seats.
前座氣袋未關閉，絕對不可放後向式嬰兒椅 — 氣袋彈出力極大，可致命。
