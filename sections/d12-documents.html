D12Documents & Legal Requirements 文件與法律要求

Sources: Road Traffic Act 1988 (ss.87, 143, 165) · Motor Vehicles (Third Party Risks) Regulations 1972 · Road Traffic Act 1988 s.47 (MOT) · Vehicle Excise and Registration Act 1994

Driving Licence 駕駛執照

  • Must have valid licence for the vehicle category (Cat B = standard car up to 3,500kg)
  • Provisional licence (learner): must display L plates (or D in Wales), be supervised by qualified driver (21+, held licence 3+ years), can't drive on motorways unsupervised by approved instructor in dual-control car
  • Full licence after passing both theory + practical tests
  • Photocard renewed every 10 years; full driving entitlement until 70, then renewed every 3 years
  • Inform DVLA of address change, name change, certain medical conditions

Insurance 保險

⚠ Driving Uninsured = Serious Offence
Minimum 6 points + £300 fine; can lead to disqualification, vehicle seized and crushed. 無保險駕駛 = 重罪。
  • Third party only — minimum legal cover; covers damage to others, not your own car
  • Third party, fire & theft — adds fire and theft of your own car
  • Comprehensive — fullest cover, includes own damage
  • Insurance recorded on MID (Motor Insurance Database) — police use ANPR to check on-the-spot
  • Must have insurance even if not driving (unless declared SORN)
  • "Insurance certificate" needed in some claims

Vehicle Excise Duty (VED / "Road Tax") 汽車稅

  • All vehicles using public roads must be taxed OR have SORN (Statutory Off Road Notification)
  • SORN = car kept off road, not used; valid until vehicle taxed, sold, scrapped or exported
  • Pay annually, 6-monthly, or monthly direct debit
  • Rates based on CO₂ emissions (newer vehicles) or engine size (older)
車稅：上路必須交稅或申報SORN（車停放唔用）。稅率睇CO₂排放（新車）或引擎大細（舊車）。

V5C Logbook 汽車登記文件

  • Vehicle registration certificate — shows registered keeper
  • Notify DVLA within 28 days of: change of address, change of name, sale/transfer of vehicle
  • Registered keeper is responsible for ensuring tax/insurance/MOT
V5C（車主登記文件）：改地址、改名或賣車，要28日內通知DVLA。

Penalty Points 違例扣分

  • Endorsements stay on licence for 4–11 years depending on offence
  • Most stay 4 years from date of offence (e.g. speeding)
  • Serious offences (drink driving, causing death): 11 years
  • 12+ points within 3 years = automatic disqualification ("totting up")
扣分：大部分留4年，嚴重（酒駕／致死）留11年。3年內夠12分會自動停牌（新牌兩年內夠6分即撤銷）。
⚠ New Drivers Act
For 2 years after passing your first driving test: if you reach 6 points, your licence is revoked. You'll need to apply for a new provisional licence and retake BOTH theory and practical tests.
考到牌後頭兩年係考牌期。如果扣夠6分，駕照即時取消，要重新考過theory同practical兩個試。
