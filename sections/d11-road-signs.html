D11Road Signs 路牌與道路標誌

Sources: Know Your Traffic Signs (DfT/DVSA, 8th edition 2019) · The Highway Code Rules 103–158 · Traffic Signs Regulations and General Directions (TSRGD) 2016

UK road signs fall into three main categories based on shape, which tells you the type of message regardless of language.

英國路牌主要分三類：三角形 = 警告、圓形 = 命令/禁止、長方形 = 資訊。記住形狀比記字更重要。
⚠ Shape = Meaning
Triangle = Warning · Circle = Order (mandatory or prohibition) · Rectangle = Information

Warning Signs 警告標誌

Red triangle with white background and black symbol. Warns you of hazards ahead.

紅色三角形，白底黑符號 — 提醒前方有危險。看到要減速、留心。

⚠ Special Triangle: STOP Sign Ahead
STOP sign is the only octagonal (8-sided) sign in the UK — you MUST come to a complete stop. STOP是唯一八角形路牌，必須完全停車。

Circles give orders. The colour tells you what type of order.

圓形 = 命令。紅圈 = 禁止，藍色實心 = 必須做。

Road Markings 路面標線

Road markings have the same legal force as signs. Learn what each pattern means.

路面標線同樣具法律效力。白線管車流，黃線管停泊。
Broken white line (centre line) — Lane separator, may cross to overtake when safe.虛白線 — 安全時可超越
Solid white line — Do NOT cross unless turning into property, passing stationary vehicle, or directed by police. 實白線 — 不可越過
Double yellow lines — No waiting at any time (loading may be permitted unless marked).雙黃線 — 任何時間禁止等候
Single yellow line — No waiting during times shown on nearby sign.單黃線 — 看牌指示時段
Double red lines (Red Route) — No stopping at any time, for anyone.雙紅線 — 任何時間、任何人都唔准停（紅線幹道 Red Route）
Single red line (Red Route) — No stopping during the times shown on nearby signs.單紅線 — 指定時段內不准停車
Zig-zag lines (white) — Approaching/leaving zebra crossing. NO stopping or overtaking.鋸齒白線 — 斑馬線兩旁，不准停車不准超車
Zig-zag lines (yellow) — Outside schools. No stopping at times shown.黃色鋸齒線 — 學校範圍不准停車

Box Junctions (Yellow Criss-Cross)

Yellow criss-cross box at junction — you MUST NOT enter unless your exit is clear. Exception: you may enter when turning right if oncoming traffic is the only thing stopping you.

黃色格仔交界 — 出口未清不可入。例外：右轉時若只是對面車流阻擋，可以入格等。

Other Important Markings

  • "SLOW" painted on road — slow down
  • Arrows on lanes — show direction of travel for that lane
  • Hatched areas (white diagonal stripes) — separates traffic streams; bordered by broken line = avoid if possible; solid line = do not enter
  • Advanced Stop Line (ASL) — extra space at traffic lights for cyclists; cars must stop before the first line
